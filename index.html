Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about farming with Harvest Heritage.

Are your farms certified organic? +

Yes. Our primary cultivation land holds full organic certification. We also work with partner farms at various stages of their organic transition, and clearly label produce accordingly.

What areas do you deliver produce to? +

We currently deliver within a 200km radius of our main farm site, with cold-chain refrigerated vehicles. We are expanding and welcome enquiries from further afield for larger wholesale orders.

How does the farm consultancy process work? +

We begin with a free initial consultation call or farm visit to assess your land, goals, and current practices. From there, we develop a tailored proposal outlining our recommended approach, timelines, and investment required.

Can I visit the farm? +

Absolutely! We run scheduled farm tours year-round, and bespoke visits for schools, corporate groups, and prospective partners can be arranged on request. Contact us to book your visit.

Do you supply to individual households? +

Yes, through our Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) box scheme. Seasonal vegetable, fruit, and herb boxes are available on weekly subscriptions for households, with collection or home delivery options.

Our Roots

A Family Farm,
Three Generations Deep

Harvest Heritage began in 1998 when Eleanor and Thomas Greenfield purchased a modest 40-acre plot with a simple vision: to grow real food in a world increasingly disconnected from it. What started as a passion project became a purpose-driven enterprise when their son Daniel joined the farm after completing his degree in Sustainable Agriculture.

Today, Harvest Heritage spans over 500 acres across three sites, employs over 60 permanent staff and seasonal workers, and supplies premium organic produce to hundreds of families and businesses across the region.

Our name reflects everything we believe in: honouring the heritage of traditional farming wisdom while harvesting a better future through innovation, sustainability, and community.

What We Stand For

Our Mission & Values

Everything we do is guided by a set of core beliefs that have shaped our farm from day one.

🌿

Regenerative Stewardship

We leave every acre in better health than we found it. Our mission is to actively restore soil vitality, biodiversity, and water cycles across all land we manage.

🤝

Community First

Farming is a community endeavour. We invest in local employment, rural education, and food access programs to ensure the benefits of the land are shared widely.

🔬

Science-Informed Practice

We honour traditional knowledge while embracing modern research. Our team continuously tests, learns, and adapts to improve outcomes for crops, soil, and ecosystem health.

🌾

Heritage Preservation

We maintain a living seed library of over 40 heirloom varieties, ensuring genetic diversity and agricultural heritage are protected for future generations.
